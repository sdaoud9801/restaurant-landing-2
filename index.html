OUR STORY FROM SEA TO TABLE


It all started with a fisherman and his son, who spent their mornings chasing the sunrise along the coast and their afternoons sharing the day’s catch with neighbors. What began as a small seaside stand grew into a passion — bringing people together over honest, ocean-fresh food.
